Overview

Engineering with Purpose

Ikenna Okonkwo is a high-impact Backend Engineer based in Nigeria, operating at the intersection of complex financial infrastructure and academic excellence. He founded Onyetech Creative Hub with a clear vision: to make world-class digital solutions accessible to every business, regardless of size.

What sets Ikenna apart is not just his technical depth — it's his philosophy. Every project he leads is approached with meticulous precision, a researcher's discipline, and an unwavering commitment to delivering results that genuinely matter to clients.

Academic & Professional

Built on Rigour & Real-World Experience

Ikenna is currently pursuing his Master of Science in Software Engineering at Quantic School of Business & Technology — one of the world's most selective online institutions. His academic journey constantly informs his engineering practice, keeping him at the forefront of modern software methodology and architecture.

Professionally, he serves as a senior engineer at Interswitch, one of Africa's most recognised FinTech companies, where he builds and maintains mission-critical merchant systems — working daily with enterprise-grade infrastructure, security hardening, cloud deployments, and high-availability systems.
