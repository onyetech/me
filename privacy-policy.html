Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  |  Onyetech Creative Hub, Nigeria

1. Who We Are

Onyetech Creative Hub ("we", "us", or "our") is a registered technology business based in Nigeria, offering web design, software development, mobile app development, graphics design, project management, and tech coaching services. Our contact email is onyetechnologies@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill out our contact or project request form (name, email address, phone number, project details)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp

We do not collect any sensitive personal data, and we do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your project enquiry or contact message
  • Send newsletters or updates you subscribed to
  • Deliver and manage services you have engaged us for
  • Improve our services and client experience

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Form Submissions & Third-Party Processors

Our website forms are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form backend service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted securely to Formspree before being delivered to us. Formspree operates under its own privacy policy available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil our service obligations. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time by replying to any newsletter email with "Unsubscribe".

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. All form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store payment information — any payments are handled through secure, independent payment channels.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at onyetechnologies@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
